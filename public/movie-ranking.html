Top 3 Movies of all time

By Daniel Onstwedder

1. Coach Carter

I loved this movie. Extremely inspiritional and motivating considering how much this man changed so many young boys' lives and most likely their futures.
9/10

2. Star Wars - Revenge Of the Sith

Unbelievably good. Contains everything you would want for a movie, cool action fight scenes, high intensity, incredible musical score, drama, emotions. All round fantastic
8.9/10

3. Inception

Very good movie. The storyline behind it is incredible that you are just sat in amazement afterwards and in awe. I remember not knowing what to think of reality after finishing it. Really keeps you on the edge of your seat and just when you think you understand what's going on, a new twist enters.
8/10
